Word problems of free inverse monoidsEquations over hyperbolic groups and semigroups (Tara Brough, NBSAN York, 18th January 2017)

Abstract: The word problem of the free inverse monoid on a finite set $X$ is context-sensitive (recognisable in linear time), but not context-free. There is a rich world of interesting language classes in between the context-free and context-sensitive, and I will discuss where the free inverse monoid word problem fits in that world.
